About Chimpanzees

Chimpanzees are highly intelligent and social primates that share approximately 98% of their DNA with humans. They are known for their problem-solving abilities and complex social structures.

Chimpanzees are native to the rainforests and savannas of Africa and are currently listed as an endangered species.

Our Refuge

Our Mission

Our refuge is dedicated to providing a safe and caring environment for rescued chimpanzees. We are committed to their well-being and strive to educate the public about the importance of conservation and protecting these incredible animals.

Conservation Efforts

We actively participate in conservation efforts to protect chimpanzees in the wild and support initiatives that promote their conservation and habitat preservation.
