Coffee Facts

  • Did you know? The average amount of caffeine in a brewed cup of coffee is around 95 milligrams.
  • Coffee is the second most traded commodity on Earth after oil.
  • It's estimated that over 2.25 billion cups of coffee are consumed every day worldwide.
  • The half-life of caffeine in the human body is typically 3 to 5 hours.
  • Caffeine is a stimulant that works by blocking the effects of adenosine, a brain chemical involved in sleep.
  • Caffeine can increase alertness, focus, and energy levels.
  • It can also improve mood and reduce fatigue.
  • However, too much caffeine can cause side effects such as anxiety, insomnia, and headaches.
  • The recommended daily caffeine intake for adults is 400 milligrams.

Effects of Caffeine

Amount (mg) Effects
<400 Alertness, focus, reduced fatigue, improved mood
400-600 Increased anxiety, jitters, insomnia
>600 Headaches, tremors, heart palpitations, anxiety, insomnia
